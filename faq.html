Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to register?

No, PosterCraft runs completely in your browser. Your data is never uploaded or stored online.

How do I export my poster?

Just click “Export PNG” on the Designer page — your design will be saved to your computer instantly.

Can I save and reopen designs later?

Yes! Use the “Save” button to store your work in localStorage, and “Load” to bring it back anytime.

Is PosterCraft free?

Yes — it’s 100% free, made for learning and creativity using front-end web technologies.

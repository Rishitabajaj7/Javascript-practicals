About PosterCraft

PosterCraft was created to make design accessible to everyone — from students creating event flyers to entrepreneurs promoting their businesses. Inspired by tools like Canva, it’s lightweight, simple, and built entirely with front-end web technologies.

Our Vision

Empowering creativity without limits. Whether it’s a digital flyer, an announcement, or a social post, PosterCraft helps you create something visually stunning in minutes.

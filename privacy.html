Privacy & Data Policy

PosterCraft is a front-end web app built for learning and design practice. It does not collect, store, or share any personal data externally. Your poster designs are saved only within your browser using localStorage.

Images uploaded for backgrounds never leave your device. When you refresh or clear your cache, those designs may be deleted from your system.

By using this website, you agree that PosterCraft is an educational tool created for demonstration purposes only.
